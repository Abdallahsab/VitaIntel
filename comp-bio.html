AI in Computational Biology

Computational biology is transforming healthcare, and AI is at its core. VitaIntel’s Computational Biology platform uses AI-ML and DataEng to uncover insights for cancer research and personalized medicine.

Unlocking Biological Data

Our AI models analyze genomic and proteomic data to identify biomarkers, achieving 92% accuracy in predicting cancer progression. This enables earlier interventions and better outcomes.

Scalable Infrastructure

Our DataEng team has built pipelines to process petabytes of biological data, reducing analysis time by 35%. This allows researchers to focus on discovery rather than data management.

Shaping the Future

We’re expanding our platform to include single-cell sequencing, aiming to uncover new therapeutic targets for rare diseases and advance precision medicine globally.
