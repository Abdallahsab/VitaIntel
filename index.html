About VitaIntel

Learn about our mission to transform healthcare with AI.

VitaIntel is a biomedical AI startup dedicated to transforming cancer care through advanced machine learning technologies. We specialize in analyzing multi-omics data — including genomics, proteomics, and clinical records — to develop predictive models for early cancer detection and personalized treatment.

Our vision is to bridge the gap between data science and real-world healthcare by building intelligent tools that assist clinicians in making faster, more accurate, and patient-specific decisions.

Founded by a team of passionate engineers and biomedical experts, we aim to redefine how cancer is understood, diagnosed, and treated — one algorithm at a time.
