Single-Cell Sequencing

VitaIntel’s Single-Cell Sequencing platform revolutionizes biomedical research by analyzing individual cells to uncover novel insights. Leveraging our expertise in AI-ML and CompBio, we’ve enabled breakthroughs in understanding diseases like breast cancer at unprecedented resolution.

Precision at the Cellular Level

Our AI-driven platform processes single-cell RNA sequencing data to identify unique gene expression profiles, achieving 90% accuracy in detecting rare cell populations. This has led to the discovery of novel biomarkers for rare diseases, transforming diagnostic capabilities.

Scalable Data Processing

Handling massive single-cell datasets is a challenge we’ve mastered. Our DataEng team developed pipelines that reduce processing time by 50%, enabling researchers to analyze millions of cells in real-time and accelerating discoveries in cancer research.

Impact and Future Goals

This platform has supported over 20 research institutions, contributing to publications in top journals. We’re now integrating multi-omics data to enhance our models, aiming to personalize therapies for complex diseases like cancer.
