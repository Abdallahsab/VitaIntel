AI-Enhanced Clinical Trials

VitaIntel's AI-Enhanced Clinical Trials platform transforms the way clinical research is conducted, making trials faster, more inclusive, and more effective. Our AI-ML and DataEng expertise drives innovation in trial design and execution.

Our AI algorithms analyze health records and genomic data to match patients with trials, reducing recruitment time by 25%. This has enabled trials with 30% more diverse cohorts, improving generalizability of results.

Optimized Trial Protocols

Using predictive models, we refine trial designs to enhance efficacy and safety. Our DataEng pipelines process real-time data, supporting adaptive trials that have boosted success rates by 20% in oncology research.

Global Impact

Deployed in over 50 trials worldwide, our platform has accelerated therapy development for diseases like cancer. We're now integrating wearable tech to monitor patient outcomes, aiming to further reduce trial durations by 15%.
