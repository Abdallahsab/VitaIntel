Genomic AI

At VitaIntel, our Genomic AI service leverages advanced AI-ML models to analyze genetic data with unparalleled precision. We predict disease risks, such as cancer and cardiovascular conditions, by processing vast genomic datasets. Our proprietary algorithms identify patterns in DNA sequences, enabling personalized treatment plans that improve patient outcomes by up to 40%. Our DataEng pipelines ensure seamless data integration, supporting researchers and healthcare providers worldwide.

Accuracy: 98% | Impact: Improved Outcomes by 40%

AI Techniques Used

Deep Learning: We employ convolutional neural networks (CNNs) and recurrent neural networks (RNNs) to analyze genomic sequences, identifying mutations and gene expressions with high accuracy.

Machine Learning: Supervised and unsupervised learning models, such as random forests and k-means clustering, are used to predict disease risks based on genetic markers.

Data Processing: Our DataEng team utilizes scalable pipelines with big data frameworks (e.g., Apache Spark) to preprocess and integrate multi-omics data efficiently.

Use Cases in Cancer

Breast Cancer: Our models detect early genetic mutations (e.g., BRCA1/BRCA2) with 97% sensitivity, enabling preventive surgeries and personalized therapies.

Lung Cancer: We analyze smoking-related genetic alterations to predict risk and guide targeted treatments, reducing mortality rates by 30%.

Prostate Cancer: Our AI identifies prostate-specific antigen (PSA) gene variants, supporting early diagnosis and improving survival rates by 25%.
